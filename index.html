What We Do & Why We Do

We believe that every warrior deserves the chance to live a dignified and fulfilled life after serving the nation. Our mission is to improve the lives of wounded and disabled warriors by providing them with access to rehabilitation, education, and other basic necessities.

We work tirelessly to identify the most pressing needs of our warriors and develop programs and initiatives to address those needs. Through our fundraising efforts and the generous support of our donors, we are able to make a positive impact on the lives of thousands of warriors every year.

Whether it's providing physical rehabilitation to an injured warrior or helping a disabled warrior receive education and training to re-enter civilian life, we are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of our warriors and their families. Together, we can do so much more for our brave warriors. Join us today and donate to Special Warrior Foundation.
